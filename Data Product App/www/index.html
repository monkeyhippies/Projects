Documentation:

Use this app if you want to calculate the power of your experiment. You can also use this app to get a ballpark estimate of how many observations you need to take, given a preferred statistical power, alpha, and estimated sigma.


Input the 4 parameters in the sidepanel:


  • 1. Number of Observations
  • 2. alpha (.05 is standard for most experiments)
  • 3. sigma (this is the population standard deviation) This should be an estimate if you don't have the data to calculate sigma
  • 4. Estimated Detectable difference (This is the estimated difference in the means of the distributions of your two competing hypotheses (null and alternative))

Main Panel Output:


  • Check to make sure the app read in the correct parameters.
  • The calculated power is displayed, and a plot showing the distributions of the two hypotheses and a line marking alpha.

Enjoy!
